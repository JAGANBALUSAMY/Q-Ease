## Getting Started

### 1. Create an Account

1. Open the Q-Ease app in your browser
2. Click **"Sign Up"**
3. Fill in your details:
   - Email address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
   - First name and last name
   - Phone number
4. Click **"Register"**
5. You'll receive a confirmation email

### 2. Log In

1. Click **"Log In"**
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **"Sign In"**

---

## Finding Organizations

### Browse Organizations

1. From the home page, you'll see a list of available organizations
2. Use the search bar to find specific organizations by:
   - Name (e.g., "City Hospital")
   - Code (e.g., "HOSP01")
   - City (e.g., "New York")

### View Organization Details

1. Click on any organization card
2. You'll see:
   - Organization name and address
   - Available queues
   - Current wait times
   - Operating hours

---

## Joining a Queue

### Method 1: Browse and Join

1. Select an organization
2. View available queues
3. Click **"Join Queue"** on your preferred queue
4. Confirm your selection
5. You'll receive a token number

### Method 2: Scan QR Code

1. Click the **QR Scanner** icon (üì∑)
2. Allow camera access
3. Point your camera at the organization's QR code
4. You'll be automatically redirected to join the queue

### What You'll Receive

After joining a queue, you'll get:
- **Token Number** (e.g., "E005")
- **Current Position** in the queue
- **Estimated Wait Time**
- **Queue Status** (Active/Paused)

---

## Tracking Your Token

### View My Tokens

1. Click **"My Tokens"** in the menu
2. You'll see all your active and past tokens
3. Each token shows:
   - Token number
   - Queue name
   - Current status
   - Position (if pending)

### Real-Time Updates

Your token status updates automatically:
- **Pending**: Waiting in queue
- **Called**: Your turn! Please proceed to the counter
- **Served**: Service completed
- **Cancelled**: Token was cancelled

### Notifications

You'll receive notifications when:
- ‚úÖ Your token is created
- üîî Your token is called
- ‚è∞ Your turn is approaching (2-3 positions away)

## Managing Your Tokens

### Cancel a Token

If you can't make it:
1. Go to **"My Tokens"**
2. Find the token you want to cancel
3. Click **"Cancel Token"**
4. Confirm cancellation

**Note:** You can only cancel tokens that are still pending.

## Tips for Best Experience

### 1. Enable Notifications
- Allow browser notifications
- You'll be alerted when your turn is near

### 2. Arrive on Time
- Check your estimated wait time
- Arrive 5-10 minutes before your turn
- If you're late, your token may be skipped

### 3. Keep the App Open
- Real-time updates work best when the app is open
- You can minimize the browser, but don't close it

### 4. Check Queue Status
- Before joining, check if the queue is active
- Paused queues won't call tokens until resumed

## Troubleshooting

### I didn't receive a notification
- Check if browser notifications are enabled
- Ensure you're logged in
- Check your internet connection

### My position isn't updating
- Refresh the page
- Check your internet connection
- The queue might be paused

### I missed my turn
- Contact the organization staff
- They may be able to call your token again
- You might need to join the queue again

### I can't join a queue
- The queue might be at capacity
- The queue might be paused
- Check if you're already in the queue

## FAQ

**Q: How many queues can I join at once?**  
A: You can join multiple queues, but only one per organization at a time.

**Q: Can I join a queue for someone else?**  
A: No, each person needs their own account and token.

**Q: What if I lose my internet connection?**  
A: Your token is saved. Reconnect to see updates.

**Q: How accurate is the wait time?**  
A: It's an estimate based on average service time. Actual time may vary.

**Q: Can I change my position in the queue?**  
A: No, positions are assigned automatically and fairly.

**Q: What if the organization closes?**  
A: All pending tokens will be cancelled automatically.
