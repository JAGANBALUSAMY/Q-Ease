## Managing Queues

### Create a New Queue

1. Click **"Queues"** in the sidebar
2. Click **"Create Queue"**
3. Fill in queue details:
   - **Name:** (e.g., "Emergency", "General Consultation")
   - **Description:** Brief description of the queue
   - **Average Time:** Estimated service time per customer (minutes)
   - **Max Tokens:** Maximum number of tokens allowed
4. Click **"Create"**

### Edit a Queue

1. Go to **"Queues"**
2. Find the queue you want to edit
3. Click the **Edit** icon (‚úèÔ∏è)
4. Update the information
5. Click **"Save Changes"**

### Pause/Resume a Queue

**To Pause:**
1. Find the queue
2. Click **"Pause Queue"**
3. Confirm the action
4. All users in the queue will be notified

**To Resume:**
1. Click **"Resume Queue"**
2. Queue will continue from where it was paused

### Delete a Queue

1. Ensure the queue is empty or paused
2. Click the **Delete** icon (üóëÔ∏è)
3. Confirm deletion

## Managing Staff

### Add Staff Member

1. Click **"Staff"** in the sidebar
2. Click **"Add Staff"**
3. Enter staff details:
   - Email
   - Name
   - Phone number
4. Assign to queues
5. Click **"Create"**
6. Staff will receive login credentials via email

### Assign Staff to Queues

1. Go to **"Staff"**
2. Select a staff member
3. Click **"Assign Queues"**
4. Select queues they should manage
5. Click **"Save"**

### Remove Staff Access

1. Find the staff member
2. Click **"Deactivate"**
3. They will no longer be able to log in

## Monitoring Performance

### View Analytics

1. Click **"Analytics"** in the sidebar
2. Select time period:
   - Today
   - This Week
   - This Month
   - Custom Range

### Key Metrics

**Queue Performance:**
- Total tokens issued
- Tokens served
- Tokens cancelled
- Average wait time
- Peak hours

**Staff Performance:**
- Tokens served per staff member
- Average service time
- Efficiency rating

**Customer Satisfaction:**
- Average rating

### Export Reports

1. Go to **"Analytics"**
2. Select the data you want
3. Click **"Export"**

## QR Code Management

### Generate Queue QR Code

1. Go to **"Queues"**
2. Select a queue
3. Click **"Generate QR Code"**
4. Download or print the QR code
5. Display it at your location

**Benefits:**
- Customers can scan and join instantly
- Reduces physical queuing
- Contactless check-in

### Display QR Codes

**Best Practices:**
- Place at entrance
- Include instructions
- Ensure good lighting
- Keep codes visible
- Update if queue changes

## Troubleshooting

### Queue Not Updating

**Check:**
- Is the queue paused?
- Is staff assigned to the queue?
- Are there any system errors?

**Solution:**
- Resume the queue
- Assign staff
- Check system logs

### Staff Can't Log In

**Check:**
- Is the account active?
- Are credentials correct?
- Is staff assigned to any queues?

**Solution:**
- Activate the account
- Reset password
- Assign to queues

### Customers Not Receiving Notifications

**Check:**
- Are notifications enabled?
- Is SMTP configured?
- Are email addresses valid?

**Solution:**
- Enable in settings
- Configure SMTP
- Verify user emails
